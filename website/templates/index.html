Four-Tier Solver Hierarchy

From fast approximations to full AC nonlinear optimization. Pick the right tool for the job.

Tier 1

Economic Dispatch

Merit-order stacking. Ignores network constraints. Microseconds.

gat opf ed grid.arrow
<1ms
Tier 2

DC-OPF

Linear approximation. Real power only. Fast N-1 screening.

gat opf dc grid.arrow
~10ms
Tier 3

SOCP Relaxation

Convex relaxation with voltage. Provable bounds. Production-ready.

gat opf socp grid.arrow
~100ms
Tier 4

AC-OPF (NLP)

Full nonlinear. IPOPT backend. Analytical Jacobian/Hessian.

gat opf ac grid.arrow
~1s
