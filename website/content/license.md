## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use GAT at my company?

**Yes, if:**
- You're using it internally for your own analysis
- You're not selling access to GAT or GAT-powered services
- You're not competing with GAT's commercial offerings

**You need a license if:**
- You want to offer GAT as a service to customers
- You're building a competing commercial product
- You're selling consulting services powered by GAT

### Can I contribute to GAT?

**Yes!** Contributions are welcome and encouraged. By contributing, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the same PolyForm Shield License.

See our [Contributing Guide](@/contributing.md) for details.

### Can I fork GAT?

**Yes**, for personal or non-commercial use. However, you cannot use a fork to create a competing commercial product or service.

### What about academic publications?

**Completely free.** Academic research, teaching, and publications are non-commercial uses covered by the license.

### Can I use GAT in open-source projects?

**Yes**, as long as the resulting project is also non-commercial. If you want to use GAT in a commercial open-source product, contact us for licensing.
